About Us

V-Jaya Industries

Founded in 1992 as Royal Die Casting, V-JAYA revolutionized aluminum die casting in our city. Starting with supplying door hardware and castings to the Indian market, our expansion into the automotive industry in the early 2000s marked a pivotal moment. From our humble beginnings with one die casting machine, we have grown to operate seven machines equipped with in-house tool making capabilities.

In addition to die casting, our versatile manufacturing capabilities span steel (cutting and stamping), plastic (injection molding), brass, zamak, wood, and ceramic products. Known for our reliability and dedication to service, we operate 24x7 to deliver products tailored precisely to your needs. Today, we proudly stand as the premier private label company in the region.
